# Store Assets Checklist

## App Icons
### iOS (App Store)
- [ ] 1024x1024 px (marketing icon, no alpha)
- [ ] Valid `AppIcon.appiconset` with all required sizes (20x20 to 1024x1024)

### Android (Play Store)
- [ ] Adaptive Icon (Foreground + Background layers)
    - Foreground: 108x108 dp (72x72 dp safe zone)
    - Background: 108x108 dp color/image
- [ ] Legacy Icon (round/square) for older Android versions
- [ ] Play Store Listing Icon: 512x512 px (32-bit PNG)

## Screenshots Plan
*Requirements: High-quality, clear status bar (or cleaned), consistent device frame (optional but recommended).*

### Essential Flows
1. **Home Screen / Venue Discovery**: Show the clean venue list or a venue card.
2. **Venue Menu**: Show the menu items, categories, and sticky headers.
3. **Cart**: Show the cart drawer or screen with items added.
4. **Checkout**: Show the payment method selection (cash/MoMo/Revolut) - NO credit card inputs.
5. **Order Received**: Show the "Order Placed" or "Received" status screen.
6. **Bell/Service**: Show the feature to call waiter (if visually distinct).

## Feature Graphic (Android)
- [ ] 1024x500 px (JPEG or 24-bit PNG, no alpha)
- [ ] Must convey "Dine-in" experience.

## Copy Rules
- **DO NOT** mention "AI" or "Artificial Intelligence".
- **DO NOT** promise "Instant" or "Guaranteed" payments (since it's handoff).
- **EMPHASIZE**:
    - "Dine-in Only"
    - "No Delivery"
    - "Touch-less ordering"
    - "Fast & Secure"

## Metadata
- **Name**: DineIn (or distinct name per country if needed)
- **Short Description**: Order from your phone at your favorite venues.
- **Full Description**:
    - Focus on convenience, speed, and safety.
    - Mention it works with partner venues in RW and MT.
