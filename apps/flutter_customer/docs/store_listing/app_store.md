## Description

Experience the future of dining with DineIn. Whether you are at a busy restaurant, a relaxed cafe, or a lively bar, DineIn lets you view the menu, order, and pay directly from your phone.

**Seamless Table Ordering**
Scan the QR code at your table to access a digital menu instantly. No need to flag down a waiter to see what's available.

**Order at Your Pace**
Browse categories, see prices, and add items to your cart with a single tap. Customize your order and checkout whenever you are ready.

**Real-Time Upates**
Stay informed with live status updates. Know exactly when your order is received, being prepared, and served to your table.

**Secure Payments**
Pay safely using your preferred method. We support Mobile Money (MoMo) in Rwanda, Revolut in Malta, and Cash options.

**Privacy First**
No account creation required for browsing. No tracking of your location. Just scan, order, and enjoy your meal.

*Note: DineIn is exclusively for in-venue table ordering. Delivery is not supported.*
