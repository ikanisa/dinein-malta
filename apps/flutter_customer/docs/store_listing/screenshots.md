# Store Screenshots Plan

**Target Device**: iPhone 15 Pro Max (6.7") & Pixel 8 Pro
**Orientation**: Portrait
**Style**: Clean device frame, solid pastel background matching brand colors, short caption at top.

## Screenshot Flow

| Order | Screen | Caption (Top) | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | **Home / Venue List** | **Discover Top Venues** | Show the venue list with clear images and specific tags (e.g., "Cafe", "Bar"). |
| 2 | **Venue Menu** | **Browse Full Menu** | Show a category like "Burgers" or "Drinks" with attractive items. |
| 3 | **Item Details** | **Add in One Tap** | Show the bottom sheet slightly open or just the "Add" button action. |
| 4 | **Cart** | **Review Your Order** | Show the cart drawer/page with multiple items and total price. |
| 5 | **Checkout** | **Secure Payment** | Show the checkout screen with MoMo/Revolut/Cash options visible. |
| 6 | **Order Status** | **Track Live Status** | Show the "Order Received" or "Served" success screen. |
| 7 | **Bell (Optional)** | **Call for Service** | Show the "Ring Bell" feature if visually distinct. |

## Caption Rules
- **Length**: Max 3-4 words per caption.
- **Tone**: Active, helpful, fast.
- **Forbidden**: "AI", "Instant Verification", "Delivery".
